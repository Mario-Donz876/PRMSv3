# QUICK START GUIDE - Reimbursement & Petty Cash

## 🚀 Getting Started in 5 Minutes

### For Staff Members

#### 💵 Reimbursement Request
**I spent money on approved items and need to be reimbursed**

1. **Get Authorization First** ✅
   - Contact your Branch Head
   - Show: item description, amount, purpose
   - Get approval before purchasing

2. **Make Your Purchase** 💳
   - Buy the items with your personal funds
   - Get an itemized receipt/invoice
   - Keep it safe!

3. **Start Reimbursement in PRMS** 📝
   - Go to `/reimbursement/add.php`
   - Fill in your branch and purchase details
   - Enter the pre-authorization information
   - Enter the invoice amount
   - Click "Create Reimbursement Request"

4. **Submit Documents** 📄
   - Copy of invoice → Procurement (GC2)
   - Original invoice → Finance (GC10A)
   - Follow prompts in the system

5. **Get Reimbursed** 💰
   - Procurement verifies goods are OK
   - Finance reviews and approves
   - Money goes to your bank account

**Time:** 1-2 weeks (depending on processing)

---

#### 💰 Petty Cash Request
**I need JMD 5,000 or less for small items**

1. **Create Request** 📝
   - Go to `/petty_cash/add.php`
   - Choose your branch
   - Enter amount (≤ JMD 5,000)
   - Explain what you're buying
   - Click "Create Petty Cash Request"

2. **Wait for Approvals** ⏳
   - Branch Head approves
   - Procurement endorses
   - Finance authorizes and **gives you cash**

3. **⏱️ YOU HAVE 24 HOURS** ⚠️
   - Buy the items immediately
   - Get itemized receipts
   - Need to complete everything in this window!

4. **Within 24 Hours - Complete These:**
   - ✅ Make your purchase
   - ✅ Return original receipt to Finance
   - ✅ Return any change to Finance
   - ✅ Let Procurement verify items

5. **Done!** ✅
   - System confirms 24-hour compliance
   - Reconciliation complete
   - Request closes

**⚠️ KEY REMINDER: 24-HOUR COUNTDOWN IS STRICT!**

---

### For Branch Heads

#### Pre-Authorize Reimbursements
1. Dashboard → Pending Approvals
2. Review reimbursement authorization request
3. Check amount and purpose
4. Click Approve or Reject
5. System forwards to Procurement

#### Authorize Petty Cash
1. Dashboard → Pending Approvals
2. Review petty cash request
3. Confirm amount and purpose
4. Click Approve
5. System routes to Procurement for endorsement

---

### For Procurement Officers (GC2)

#### Verify Goods/Services (Reimbursement)
1. Dashboard → Verification Queue
2. Click reimbursement request
3. Check invoice details
4. Inspect goods or confirm service rendered
5. If OK: Click "Verify" → marks as satisfactory
6. If not OK: Reject with reason

#### Endorse Petty Cash
1. Dashboard → Petty Cash Queue
2. Review branch head authorization
3. Click "Endorse" to approve
4. Routes to Finance for final authorization

#### Verify Petty Cash Goods (Within 24 Hours)
1. After cash disbursed, dashboard shows verification queue
2. Review what staff purchased
3. Inspect items or confirm service
4. Record condition (satisfactory/defective/incomplete)
5. Mark as verified within deadline

---

### For Finance Officers (GC10A)

#### Process Reimbursement
1. Dashboard → Reimbursement Queue
2. Review procurement verification
3. Check original invoice amount
4. Verify funds are available
5. Click "Approve" to process payment
6. Payment goes to staff account

#### Disburse Petty Cash
1. Dashboard → Petty Cash Authorization Queue
2. Review procurement endorsement
3. Verify amount ≤ JMD 5,000
4. Click "Disburse"
5. Mark disbursement time (24-hour clock starts now)
6. Hand cash to staff

#### Monitor 24-Hour Reconciliation
1. Dashboard shows petty cash deadline countdowns
2. When deadline approaches, alerts appear
3. Wait for reconciliation submission (invoice + change)
4. Verify amounts reconcile (purchase + change = total)
5. If complete, mark reconciled
6. If incomplete, flag for investigation

## ⏰ 24-HOUR RULE for Petty Cash

### What Is It?
After you receive petty cash, you have exactly 24 hours to:
1. Make your purchases ✅
2. Return receipts to Finance ✅
3. Return any change to Finance ✅
4. Have Procurement verify goods ✅

### Why So Strict?
- Prevents unaccounted cash
- Ensures accountability
- Protects organization funds
- Quick turnaround = better control

### Example Timeline
```
3:00 PM Friday - You receive JMD 5,000 cash (START)
       │
       ├─ 3-6 PM - Go shopping, get receipts
       │
       ├─ 6 PM - Back to office with purchases
       │
       ├─ 6-7 PM - Submit receipts & change to Finance
       │
       ├─ 7 PM - Finance reconciles (spent 4,850, change 150)
       │
       ├─ Evening - Procurement inspects items
       │
3:00 PM Saturday - DEADLINE (24 hours later)
       │
       └─ All above must be completed or FLAG raised!
```

### What If I Miss The Deadline?
- System flags as overdue
- Investigation required
- You explain the delay
- Multiple violations may affect privileges
- So... don't miss it! ⏱️

## 💡 Quick Tips

### For Reimbursement
- ✅ Always get pre-authorization in writing (email OK)
- ✅ Keep ALL original receipts
- ✅ Don't spend more than authorized
- ✅ Submit promptly - don't wait weeks
- ✅ Make sure receipts are itemized (not just total)
- ✅ Check your bank account for payment

### For Petty Cash
- ⏱️ SET A REMINDER for 23-hour mark
- 📱 Use your phone calendar
- 📄 Organize receipts IMMEDIATELY after buying
- 💵 Return change same day as purchase
- 📋 Keep receipts together
- 🔔 Watch for system alerts

## ❓ Common Questions

### Reimbursement

**Q: Can I buy without authorization?**
A: No - you'll be reimbursed only if pre-authorized.

**Q: What if invoice is more than authorized?**
A: You'll be reimbursed for the authorized amount only.

**Q: How long does reimbursement take?**
A: Usually 1-2 weeks after all approvals.

**Q: What if goods are defective?**
A: Procurement will reject and reimbursement won't proceed.

### Petty Cash

**Q: What if I can't spend it all?**
A: Return unused balance to Finance within 24 hours.

**Q: Can I get an extension?**
A: No - the 24-hour rule is firm and mandatory.

**Q: What happens if I'm late?**
A: Investigation is triggered and you must explain.

**Q: Can I request petty cash again tomorrow?**
A: Yes, each request is separate with its own 24-hour deadline.
