# Quick Start: Pending Actions on Your Dashboard

## What's New? 🎉

Starting today, **all dashboards show a "Pending Actions" section** at the top. This means you can see everything you need to do without leaving your dashboard!

## Where Do I Find It?

When you log into your dashboard, look for the **"⏳ Pending Actions"** section right after the KPIs and alerts.

## What Will I See?

Your pending actions section will show two types of items:

### 1. **Approvals Awaiting Your Action**
Things waiting for you to approve or review:
- Request number
- Who requested it
- How much it costs
- When it was submitted
- ➡️ **Click "Review" to see it**

### 2. **Workflow Actions Required**
Things you need to do as part of the workflow:
- Request number
- Current status (like "RFQ Letter Available" or "Quotes Pending Review")
- What action is needed next
- ➡️ **Click "View Request" to take action**

## How It Works

1. **You log in** to your dashboard
2. **You see "Pending Actions"** section with items for YOUR role
3. **You click the action button** to review/approve/complete
4. **No extra navigation needed** - everything is right there!

## What Statuses Trigger Actions?

If a request is at one of these statuses, you'll see an action item:

| Status | What You See | Your Action |
|--------|-----|-----|
| RFQ_LETTER_AVAILABLE | Generate RFQ Letters | Create and send RFQ to vendors |
| QUOTE_REVIEW_PENDING | Review Quotes | Choose best quote |
| QUOTE_APPROVED | Create Commitment | Request finance to approve |
| COMMITMENT_APPROVED | Create Purchase Order | Generate PO from GFMS |
| PROCUREMENT_STAGE | Create RFQ | Set up bid process |
| EVALUATION_STAGE | Evaluate RFQ | Score bids |
| COMMITTEE_RECOMMENDED | GC Approval Required | Give final approval |
| GC_APPROVED | Ready for Award | Award contract |

## FAQ

**Q: Why don't I see any pending actions?**  
A: Great! That means you're all caught up. All pending items for your role have been handled.

**Q: Can I filter or sort the pending actions?**  
A: Right now, items are sorted by date. We're working on more filtering options for future versions.

**Q: What if I miss something on my dashboard?**  
A: Items will stay visible until you take action. You can always check your dashboard anytime.

**Q: Do I need to remember my role restrictions?**  
A: Nope! The dashboard automatically shows only what's relevant to YOUR role. You'll never see items you shouldn't have access to.

**Q: Can I bulk approve items?**  
A: Not yet, but we're planning that for the next version!

**Q: Does this change require new permissions?**  
A: No! It uses your existing role and permissions. No admin action needed.
